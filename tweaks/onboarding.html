agee

Tweak any website. Pop the bar, speak or type, and the page changes. Your tweaks save and re-apply on revisit.

1

Pin agee

Click the puzzle-piece icon in the toolbar → pin agee for quick access.

2

Add your model key

agee uses your own API key — calls go straight from your browser. Paste it below.

3

Open the bar on any page

Click the floating agee pill, or press your shortcut. Then hold the mic (or Space) to talk, release when done — or just type. Press Enter to apply.

4

Save & reuse

Like a tweak? Hit Save — it re-applies whenever you revisit the site. Toggle or delete saved tweaks from the bar. No login needed; sign in later to sync and share.

Stored locally in this browser only. Get one at console.anthropic.com. (ChatGPT-subscription sign-in is on the roadmap.)
Used for speech recognition. Auto-detect (any language) is on the roadmap via cloud transcription.
Heads-up: agee can't run on browser-internal pages (Settings, Extensions, the new-tab page). Open a normal website to start tweaking.
